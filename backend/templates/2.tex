\section*{Research Experience}
\textbf{Tuning of Thermal Conductivity of Monolayer Gallium Nitride Nano-ribbon: An Equilibrium Molecular Dynamics Study}\\
Equilibrium Molecular Dynamics simulations using the Stillinger-Weber potential were deployed to compute the thermal conductivity of nanometer-sized zigzag monolayer GaN nano-ribbons (NR). The dependence of thermal transport on variation of length and width was explored. Additionally, the impact of aluminum and indium doping on the thermal conductivity of monolayer GaN-NR was investigated.
